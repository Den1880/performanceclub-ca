The Performance
Club.

Mon — Fri / 6:00am — 8:00pm

We've packed our private fitness facility with top-tier gear to cover every training style. Get your heavy lifting done with our multi-functional power rig, Smith machine, and a full suite of Olympic bars, plates, and dumbbells.

If cardio is your focus, jump on the Peloton Bikes, Treads, or Row for a world-class session. For those who like to keep it functional, we've got TRX, battle ropes, plyo boxes, and GORUCK weighted rucks, plus a dedicated reset zone with yoga mats, a ballet barre, and a recovery fridge to cool down and fuel up.
